Module 7: Managing Financial Risks

Insurance Types

  • Health Insurance: Provides coverage for medical expenses. It's crucial to protect against high healthcare costs and ensure access to necessary treatments. Check out this article on Healthcare.gov for more details.
  • Life Insurance: Offers financial protection to your beneficiaries in the event of your death. This ensures that your loved ones are financially secure. For a deeper understanding, read this Investopedia article.
  • Disability Insurance: Replaces a portion of your income if you become unable to work due to a disability. It's essential for safeguarding your financial well-being. Watch this video on YouTube to learn more about its importance.

Risk Mitigation Strategies

Identifying potential financial risks is the first step in managing them. Here are some common strategies:

  • Diversification: Spread investments across various asset classes to reduce exposure to any single risk.
  • Emergency Fund: Maintain savings to cover unexpected expenses, preventing the need to incur debt during emergencies.
  • Regular Financial Reviews: Assess your financial situation regularly to identify risks and adjust strategies accordingly.
